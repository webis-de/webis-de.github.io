Synopsis

The Wikidata vandalism corpus 2015 (WDVC-15) is a corpus for the evaluation of automatic vandalism detectors for Wikidata. For research purposes the corpus can be used free of charge.

Download

You can access the WDVC-15 corpus on Zenodo.

If you use the dataset in your research, please send us a copy of your publication. We kindly ask you to cite the corpus via [bib]. If you additionally want to link to the dataset, please use the dataset's [doi] for a stable link.
