Synopsis

The Webis-WebSeg-20 dataset comprises 42,450 crowdsourced segmentations for 8,490 web pages from the Webis-Web-Archive-17. Segmentations were fused from the segmentations of five crowd workers each. If you use this dataset in your research, please cite it using this paper. [download]
