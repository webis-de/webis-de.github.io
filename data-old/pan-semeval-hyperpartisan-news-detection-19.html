Synopsis

The PAN-SemEval-Hyperpartisan-News-Detection-19 has been developed in cooperation with Factmata for the PAN @ SemEval 2019 Task on Hyperpartisan news detection. More information on the task can be found on the task's web site.

Resources

You can access the PAN-SemEval-Hyperpartisan-News-Detection-19 corpus on Zenodo.

If you use the dataset in your research, please send us a copy of your publication. We kindly ask you to cite the corpus via [bib]. If you additionally want to link to the dataset, please use the dataset's [doi] for a stable link.
