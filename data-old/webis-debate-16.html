Webis-Debate-16

  • Download
  • People

Synopsis

A collection of phrases classified as argumentative or non-argumentative. The phrases have the topics culture, economy, education, environment, free speech, health, international, law , politics, religion, science, society and sport.

Download

You can access the Webis-Debate-16 corpus on Zenodo.

If you use the dataset in your research, please send us a copy of your publication. We kindly ask you to cite the corpus via [bib]. If you additionally want to link to the dataset, please use the dataset's [doi] for a stable link.

Research

The classifications and the phrases were seperated by tabs in text files. There are in total 10.846 argumentative phrases and 5556 non-argumentative ones.
