Synopsis

This corpus provides the simulation data mining community with a collection of 14641 bridge models and simulated behavior.

Download

You can access the Webis-SDMbridge-12 corpus on Zenodo.

If you use the dataset in your research, please send us a copy of your publication. If you additionally want to link to the dataset, please use the dataset's [doi] for a stable link.

Research

This corpus is linked with the digital engineering project. All accepted publications and related information is given there.
