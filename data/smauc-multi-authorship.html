Synopsis

The dataset is a unified and cleaned combination of eight publicly available, human-labeled web-page corpora used for "main content extraction" (boilerplate removal). It comprises annotated HTML pages (with ground-truth "main content" vs boilerplate) from diverse genres, to support systematic evaluation and benchmarking of content-extraction algorithms.
