Synopsis

An English corpus for studying the retrieval of the best counterargument to an argument. It contains 6753 pairs of argument and best counterargument from the online debate portal idebate.org, along with different experiment files with up to millions of candidate pairs. In case you publish any results related to the ArguAna Counterargs corpus, please cite our upcoming ACL 2018 paper on counterarguments.
