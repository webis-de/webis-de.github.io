Synopsis

An English benchmark dataset for studying argument relevance. It contains 32 rankings as well a ground-truth argument graph with more than 30,000 argument units. In addition, we provide the source code to reproduce our ranking experiments based on the dataset. In case you publish any results related to the Webis-ArgRank-17 dataset, please cite our EACL 2017 paper on argument relevance.
