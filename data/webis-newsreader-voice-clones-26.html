Synopsis

The Webis Newsreader Voice Clones 2026 dataset is a collection of 32 audio clips that represent voice clones of the famous newsreaders Andrea Mitchell, Carl Nasman, Lester Holt, and Sophie Raworth. These audio clips were used as experimental stimuli to simulate voice clone attacks and investigate humans' ability to detect these spoofed voices in a study described by the CHIIR'26 paper "Does Cognitive Load Affect Human Accuracy in Detecting Voice-Based Deepfakes?".
