Synopsis

Deep Text Analytics is a research collaboration between Adobe Systems and the Webis Research Group at the Bauhaus-Universität Weimar. The goal of this collaboration is to develop and to evaluate sophisticated text mining and machine learning technology that enables costumers to make better decisions when dealing with textual content. More specifically, we focus on algorithmic challenges that are to be tackled if for professional information processing tasks intelligent support technology shall be provided. Examples for such tasks include the organization and maintenance of corporate documentation, profiling of sequential business data, visual inspection of revision histories, or filling of online forms.
