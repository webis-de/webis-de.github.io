Synopsis

Retrieval models are based on a linguistic theory and can be considered as heuristics that operationalize the probability ranking principle: "Given a query q, the ranking of documents according to their probabilities of being relevant to q leads to the optimum retrieval performance." (Robertson, 1997). To overview and compare the characteristics of well-known retrieval models we have developed an interactive map. [demo]
