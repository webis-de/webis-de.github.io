Synopsis

InfexBA is a cooperative project of the German Federal Ministry of Education and Research (BMBF). The aim of the project is to develop Information Extraction (IE) technologies for business intelligence tools and to make these technologies available in powerful software components. State of the art Algorithms from Information Retrieval and Information Extraction will be used to automatically solve the following problems: (1) sentiment analysis of user generated content and (2) analysis of reports on the development of markets and organizations. Further information can be found on the official project page which is maintained by the University of Paderborn. [demo: sentiment detection]
