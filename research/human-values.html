Synopsis

Human values, such as being self-directed or protecting close ones, are commonly accepted answers to why something is desirable in the ethical sense. Human values are thus essential both in real-world argumentation and theoretical argumentation frameworks. In our research we study the detection and analysis of the (often implicit) human values behind natural language texts. As part of our research, we co-coordinated the ValuesML project together with the Joint Research Centre of the European Commission. [data] [demos: argument analysis, article analysis] [events]
