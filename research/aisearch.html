Synopsis

AIsearch is a meta search engine that offers a convenient interface for Web-based search; it integrates algorithms for the formation, labeling, and visualization of result categories. The category formation relies on a new clustering algorithm (MajorClust) and employs strategy patterns to make term weighting schemes, similarity measures, clustering algorithms, and cluster validity measures interchangeable at runtime.
