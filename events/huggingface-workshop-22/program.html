The workshop will consist of the first two tutorials of the Hugging Face Education Toolkit:

1. A Tour through the Hugging Face Hub [slides][code]

  • Explore the over 30,000 models shared in the Hub.
  • Learn efficient ways to find the right model and datasets for your own task.
  • Learn how to contribute and work collaboratively in your ML workflows.

2. Build and Host Machine Learning Demos [slides][code]

  • Explore ML demos created by the community.
  • Build a quick demo for your machine learning model in Python using the gradio library.
  • Learn how to contribute and work collaboratively in your ML workflows.
  • Host the demos for free with Hugging Face Spaces.
  • Add your demo to the Hugging Face org for your class or conference.

Omar Espejel is Machine Learning Developer at Hugging Face. He co-founded the Latin American School of AI where he volunteers to teach state-of-the-art deep learning. He is a science graduate from the University of London (London School of Economics). Omar is also the main host of the "We Decentralize Tech" podcast, a premier Spanish-language Spotify podcasts focused on cutting edge technology.
