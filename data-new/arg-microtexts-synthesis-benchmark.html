Synopsis

130 logos-oriented and 130 pathos-oriented benchmark arguments for 10 topics, manually synthesized by 26 experts based on a pool of argumentative discourse units from the Arg-Microtexts corpus. In case you publish any results related to the this benchmark data, please cite our COLING 2018 paper on argumentation synthesis.

Access

We kindly ask you to cite the dataset as [bib]. If you want to link the dataset, please use the dataset permalink [doi].
