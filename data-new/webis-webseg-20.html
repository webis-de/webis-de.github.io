Synopsis

The Webis-WebSeg-20 dataset comprises 42,450 crowdsourced segmentations for 8,490 web pages from the Webis-Web-Archive-17. Segmentations were fused from the segmentations of five crowd workers each.

Access

We kindly ask you to cite the dataset as [bib]. If you want to link the dataset, please use the dataset permalink [doi].

  • Download the dataset from Zenodo or from the Internet Archive
  • Find the related metadata at Google.
